“I'm afraid of making the biggest mistake of my life without even realizing it.”

What's holding you back

  • Mortgage jargon: rates, down payment, CMHC… no one explains it simply.
  • The fear of overpaying or running into hidden defects.
  • Bidding wars: you lose 3 offers and start to lose heart.

What I do

  • We start from your real budget: I connect you with the right mortgage broker before we visit.
  • Analysis of sold comparables — you know if the price holds up.
  • A clear offer strategy, inspection built into the plan, zero pressure.

“How do I know I'm being given the real price — not just the one that wins the listing?”

What's holding you back

  • The fear of underpricing (leaving money on the table) or overpricing (sitting on the market).
  • Agents who inflate the price to sign you, then push you to drop it after 2 months.
  • Showings that disrupt your daily life with nothing to show for it.

What I do

  • A documented evaluation, backed by real sales in your area — not a promise.
  • A full marketing plan: pro photos, video, 3D tour, Centris + social distribution.
  • Filtered, grouped showings with feedback after each one.

“What if I sell but can't find anything to buy back in time?”

What's holding you back

  • The nightmare of a “double move” or two mortgages at once.
  • Syncing the sale date and the purchase date without getting stuck.
  • Understanding the sale-conditional clause and bridge financing.

What I do

  • One single timeline that coordinates both transactions, week by week.
  • Sale and purchase conditions aligned to protect you on both sides.
  • Access to a bridge-financing network if the dates don't line up perfectly.
1 single point of contact for both files Plan both my dates →

“I don't want to buy a building — I want to buy a return.”

What's holding you back

  • Listings showing a “gross” return that melts away once real expenses are counted.
  • Tenants in place, leases, the Tribunal administratif du logement.
  • Spotting the good buildings before they hit the public market.

What I do

  • A real net-return calculation: taxes, maintenance, vacancy, financing.
  • Reading the leases and rent history before the offer.
  • Watching off-market opportunities from my broker network.

“The Quebec process is different from back home — where do I start?”

What's holding you back

  • Little or no Canadian credit history: banks hesitate.
  • You don't yet know the neighbourhoods or their real value.
  • The notary, the inspection, the promise to purchase: it's all new.

What I do

  • I explain every step of the Quebec process, in your language (FR / EN).
  • Neighbourhood choices based on your lifestyle, commute and kids.
  • Financial partners used to newcomer files.
